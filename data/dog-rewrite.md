DESIGN GOAL

This life should NOT feel like:

“aww cute dog simulator”

It should feel:

instinctive
emotional
survival-focused
relational
attachment-heavy

Main themes:

loyalty
dependence
fear
territoriality
attachment
hunger
pack behavior
trust
instinct vs compassion

The player should feel:

less agency than human life
more emotional immediacy
more reactive choices

This is IMPORTANT philosophically:
The game should subtly show:

different forms of life create different moral limitations.
