DESIGN GOAL

This life should feel:

stressful
morally gray
intellectually uncomfortable
realistic to college students
tied to Buddhism/Jainism/Hinduism concepts WITHOUT becoming a quiz app

A “good” answer should often:

hurt materially
create uncertainty
sacrifice comfort
challenge ego/attachment

A “bad” answer should often:

help short-term
feel emotionally justified
still carry karmic weight

A perfect run should feel VERY difficult.
