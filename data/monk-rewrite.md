Design Goal

Monk life should be the hardest one because the danger is not obvious evil.

It should feel like:

ego hidden inside spirituality
compassion vs detachment
wisdom vs performance
helping vs controlling
silence vs avoidance
“being spiritual” becoming an identity

Main themes:

Buddhist nonattachment
dukkha
ego
compassion
impermanence
spiritual pride
liberation
