Design Goal

Micro-organism life should be:

funny
weird
short-feeling but still meaningful
about limited agency
about how not every life has the same moral freedom
a “cosmic troll” life, but still tied to karma/rebirth

This life should feel like:

“Bro I barely exist… why am I being judged?”
