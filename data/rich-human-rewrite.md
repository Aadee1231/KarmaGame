Design Goal

Rich Human life should feel like:

power is morally dangerous
“good” actions can still feed ego
consequences happen far away from you
wealth creates comfort, control, and attachment
the hardest thing is not giving money — it is giving up power

Main themes:

privilege
charity vs ego
exploitation
public image
control
dharma/responsibility
karma at scale
